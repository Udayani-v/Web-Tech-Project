USE ECO-FRIENDLY METHODS FIRST & CHEMICALS AS A LAST RESORT!!
Sustainable low cost methods
Cutural Practises
Deep ploughing during dry summer season. Use of pest resistant varieties. Inter cropping and crop rotation. Weeding and harrowing.
Biological Methods
Use of pheromone traps, predators, parasites and bio-pestcides.
Mechanical Methods
Destruction of insects by hand picking and using hand nets. Use of light traps and sticky traps.
Bio Pesticides
Microbial Pesticides: Trichoderma, NPV, Pseudomonas etc. Botanical Pesticides: Based on Neem, Garlic etc.
Precautions for using chemical pesticides
Pesticides carrying red triangle are more toxic (Followed by yellow, blue and green triangles)
Use doses as per the package of practises otherwise as per indication on the label
While spraying pesticides, wear full protective clothing such as mask, gloves etc.
Spray pesticides early morning or late evening and take bath after spray.
Spray in the direction of wind and keep away from pesticides
Keep pesticides, sprayers and mixing utensils locked away from children and pets
Go for generic name of pesticide and always check expiry date on label.
In case of pesticide poisoning, take along pesticide container/leaflet to a doctor.
