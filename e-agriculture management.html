ABOUT
	

	In the present scenario of a technological world, when all tasks are made 
        easy by incorporating various aspects of technology, the life of farmers 
	can be made less complicated by introducing a new facet of agriculture.
	E-Agriculture Management aims at online buying and selling of crops,
	analysis of the user requirements to predict the crop in demand, suggestions
	on the techniques and equipments to be used, keeping a track of the crop
	status and regular monitoring by experts.The information provided by the farmer
	serves as a data which is analysed accordingly.
